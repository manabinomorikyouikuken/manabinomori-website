FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need any accounting knowledge to start?
No. Day 1 starts from the very beginning — "What is bookkeeping?" No prior knowledge is required. If you do have accounting experience from your home country, the IFRS references throughout the workbook will help you connect what you already know to the Japanese system.
Do I need to read Japanese?
No Japanese reading ability is required. All questions and explanations are in English. Japanese accounting terms are shown with furigana (reading guide), katakana, and rōmaji so you can learn them at your own pace alongside the English content.
What files will I receive?
You will receive an HTML file (the interactive question workbook). Open it in any browser — Safari, Chrome, Firefox — on any device. No app installation is required. The file works completely offline.
Can I use it on my smartphone?
Yes. The workbook is fully responsive and works on smartphones, tablets, and PCs. Your answer history is saved in the browser, so you can continue where you left off on the same device.
Is the exam conducted in Japanese?
Yes — the actual exam (日商簿記検定3級) is in Japanese. This workbook teaches you the accounting concepts and trains you on the 90 most important questions, all in English. By learning the Japanese terms through the trilingual notation system, you will be able to read and answer the Japanese-language exam questions confidently.
Can I really pass in 30 days?
If you study 45 minutes a day consistently and follow the 30-day plan, you will reach the knowledge level needed to pass. Consistently scoring 70%+ on the Week 4 mock exams is a reliable sign of readiness. Results vary by individual, but the curriculum is designed for exactly this goal.
